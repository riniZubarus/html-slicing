Support the Women's Front with a gift

By giving support to the Women's Front, you contribute to the work for women's liberation locally, nationally and internationally.

The Women's Front is a member organization independent of party politics. We run extensive and versatile women's political work; both locally, nationally and internationally. Women's liberation is not possible without economic independence and a well-developed welfare society.

Our goal is a society where everyone can live out their identity, love, sexuality and their way of being a woman based on equality and consent, without the risk of being discriminated against and/or harassed.

We carry out broad women's political work, and work on topics such as self-determined abortion, prostitution, financial independence, porn, the six-hour working day, international solidarity, and much more.

To continue our important work, we are completely dependent on the support of members and friends. By donating a monthly amount to Kvinnefronten, you help make our economy more stable and predictable - thank you!

About the Women's Front

The Women's Front sees the organization of women as both a goal and a means.Women can only change the world if we come together and fight together.Through discussions and community, we can understand our own lives and put our own experiences in a larger context.

We have local teams in many places in the country, and it is possible to be as active or as little as you want.
